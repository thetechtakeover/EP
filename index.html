What is life really about?


Pursuing technology was never a thought of mine. Why? because I thought technology were for the "NERD" students who earned straight A's. After a chance conversation with a childhood friend, who is currently a Senior Software Engineer, the seed was planted. The curiosity grew as time passed on but with no real directions. Being excited, I searched for engineering careers that paid high yearly salaries and then applied to Florida Atlantic University in hopes of getting accepted. I was accepted into the engineering program with a concentration in civil engineering but ultimately decided against attending because I had no interest in civil engineering.
